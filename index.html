FAQ

How do I get started?

Send an email to jemgdev@gmail.com with the subject "Owner creation request for Logifly for app YourAppName". We'll create your owner account and send you credentials within 24 hours.

What is an "app" in Logifly?

An app is a project you manage through Logifly. Each app has its own user pool, roles, and webhook configuration. One user can belong to multiple apps with different roles in each.

Do I need to manage AWS infrastructure?

No. Logifly runs on AWS but you don't need an AWS account. Everything is managed for you — Cognito, Lambda, DynamoDB. You just call the REST API.

What languages are supported for emails?

English and Spanish. You choose the language per user when pre-registering them.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. Just reach out to jemgdev@gmail.com and we'll adjust your plan. No lock-in.
